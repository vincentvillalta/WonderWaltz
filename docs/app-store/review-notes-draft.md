# App Store Review Notes -- WonderWaltz

## In-App Purchase Model

WonderWaltz uses a single consumable in-app purchase: "Trip Unlock" at $9.99.

Each $9.99 unlock applies to one trip planning session. Once applied, the credit is
consumed and cannot be reused for a different trip. Users can purchase multiple unlocks
for multiple trips.

## How It Works

1. Users create a trip for free and receive a complimentary Day 1 preview
2. To see the full multi-day plan, users purchase a Trip Unlock ($9.99)
3. The unlock is tied to that specific trip and cannot be transferred
4. Users can create new trips and purchase additional unlocks as needed
